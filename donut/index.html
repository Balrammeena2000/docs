# Donut

Donut是一个连接Steem Blockchain和Polkadot的跨链桥，旨在在Polkadot上构建Steem 2.0。

Donut is a cross-chain bridge of Steem Blockchain and Polkadot, aiming at building Steem 2.0 on Polkadot。
